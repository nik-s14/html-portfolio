BEST MOVIES OF ALL TIME

These movies are considered to be the best films of all time

In this web page, I'll mention some of the nest films I've watched over the course of the last couple of years.
I will be including a general summary, the genre, the IMDB rating and the streaming sites you can use to watch the mentioned films.

1. Shawshank Redemption

Drama

Summary: Over the course of several years, two convicts form a friendship, seeking consolation and, eventually, redemption through basic compassion.
IMDB rating: 9.3/10
Streaming sites: Amazon prime

2. The Green Mile

Crime, drama, fantasy

Summary: A tale set on death row in a Southern jail, where gentle giant John possesses the mysterious power to heal people's ailments. When the lead guard, Paul, recognizes John's gift, he tries to help stave off the condemned man's execution.
IMDB rating: 8.6/10
Streaming sites: HULU, Prime, Netflix

3. Superbad

Comedy

Summary: Two co-dependent high school seniors are forced to deal with separation anxiety after their plan to stage a booze-soaked party goes awry.
IMDB rating: 7.6/10
Streaming sites: Netflix

4. Gone Girl

Crime, Mystery, Thriller

Summary: With his wife's disappearance having become the focus of an intense media circus, a man sees the spotlight turned on him when it's suspected that he may not be innocent.
IMDB rating: 8.1/10
Streaming sites: Netflix
